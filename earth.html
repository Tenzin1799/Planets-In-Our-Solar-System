Earth!

  • Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only place known in the universe where life has originated and found habitability.

  • Earth is the only planet known to sustain liquid surface water, with ocean water extending over 70.8% of the planet, making it an ocean world.

  • Earth, like most other bodies in the Solar System, formed 4.5 billion years ago from gas in the early Solar System. During the first billion years of Earth's history, the ocean formed and then life developed within it.
